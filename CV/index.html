Profile

Hello! My name is Nikita. I'm experienced and reliable product and project manager, combineing skills of product and business management and technical background of front-end developer.

I'm happy to work with smart, open-minded people and to use modern technologies and methodologies. I like to propose and implement hypotheses, influence the development of a project, design a product, plan development paths, experiment, implement new opportunities, discuss new features come up with ways out of hopeless business situations, analyse.

I have:

  • Data-driven and outcome focused mindset. Analytical mind, ability to catch trends and draw conclusions based on numbers
  • Forward-thinking and taking the initiative to anticipate the needs of team
  • Ability to take responsibility and be accountable for results
  • Ability to critically analyse the success (or un-success) of my solutions and iterate
  • Excellent communications and interpersonal skills, empathy and ability to collaborate and communicate within all levels of the company, clients and contractors
  • Good sense of design
  • Technical background of frontend developer
  • Good written & verbal communication skills in English (Upper-intermediate now, but progressing)

I can:

  • Form and maintain a product development roadmap based on a deep understanding of new customers’ needs and wants, collect and analyse their requirements and product feedback, make data-driven decisions about the direction to grow
  • Analyse product metrics to define a better approach in delivering products with superior quality and with highly desired features set
  • Keep up to date and prioritise features in the product backlog
  • Monitor competitors in an assessment analysis of the strengths and weaknesses, review competitor’s products
  • Define product features, write clear detailed requirements, based on data, customer development and product expertise
  • Lead team (managed different teams 3-20 members) and mentor colleagues
  • Facilitate communication throughout the development process inside the company, including engineering and design teams
  • Manage projects delivery based on agile methodologies

Most experienced in industries: fintech, logistics, B2C services.
Also interested in gaining experience in: crypto, web3, B2B services.
