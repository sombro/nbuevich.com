Swapcard + ExpoPlatform + Grip account for $639,100 out of $756,400 (84.5%). The remaining 7 partners share just 15.5%. Portfolio diversification is a key strategic priority.

📝 Key Conclusions

🔴 Risks:

· Near-total dependence on Swapcard (47%). Any churn there is catastrophic.

· Strong seasonality with unexplained dips (Jul, Mar).

· 6 of 10 partners contribute under 5% of revenue each — long tail that isn't pulling weight.

· Oct 2025 data integrity concern — needs verification.

🟢 Opportunities:

· Cloud Touch and Amego show premium deal values — invest in growing their pipelines.

· ExpoPlatform proved capable of a 4× monthly surge (Jan 2026). Understand the trigger and replicate.

· April 2026 all-time high suggests strong upward momentum heading into summer 2026.

· Grip's 12-month consistency is a foundation — could serve as a benchmark for other partners.
